Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when using WatchNextAI, including:

  • Account information (username, email)
  • Movie, TV show, and anime preferences
  • Search queries and viewing history
  • Chat interactions with our AI chatbot

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide personalized movie, TV show, and anime recommendations
  • Improve our machine learning algorithms
  • Enhance user experience and service functionality
  • Respond to your inquiries through the AI chatbot

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. User preferences and interaction data are stored securely and used solely for improving recommendation accuracy.

4. Third-Party Services

WatchNextAI integrates with third-party services:

  • TMDB (The Movie Database): Provides comprehensive movie and TV show data including titles, descriptions, poster images, ratings, cast information, and release dates. All movie and TV content displayed on WatchNextAI is sourced from TMDB. Usage subject to TMDB Terms of Use and TMDB Privacy Policy.
  • Jikan API (MyAnimeList): Unofficial MyAnimeList API providing anime titles, descriptions, images, ratings, episode counts, and related metadata. All anime content displayed on WatchNextAI is sourced from Jikan/MyAnimeList. Data collection subject to MyAnimeList's terms and privacy practices.
  • Groq AI: Powers our AI chatbot functionality enabling conversational interactions about movies, TV shows, and anime. Chat messages are processed through Groq's API to generate responses. Subject to Groq's Terms of Service and data handling practices.

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, maintain user sessions, and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Export your data

8. Children's Privacy

WatchNextAI is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
